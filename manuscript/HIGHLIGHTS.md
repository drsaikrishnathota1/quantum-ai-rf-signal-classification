# Highlights

- A reproducible workflow evaluates RF modulation classifiers under degraded spectrum conditions.
- Classical ML, raw-IQ CNN, and simulated quantum feature-map kernel baselines are compared transparently.
- Robustness-drop analysis identifies model failure modes under jamming, low SNR, multipath, and frequency offset.
- The current evidence does not support a quantum-advantage claim; it supports careful robustness-aware benchmarking.
- The repository provides scripts, metrics, figures, and manuscript-ready tables for reproducibility.
