# Highlights

- A reproducible workflow evaluates RF modulation classifiers under degraded spectrum conditions.
- Full RadioML2016.10A GPU validation covers 220,000 public benchmark examples.
- Classical ML, raw-IQ CNN, and simulated quantum feature-map kernel baselines are compared transparently.
- Robustness-drop analysis identifies severe failure modes under jamming, low SNR, multipath, and frequency offset.
- Results do not support a quantum-advantage claim; they support cautious robustness-aware benchmarking.
